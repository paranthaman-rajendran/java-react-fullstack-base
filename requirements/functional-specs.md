# Lending Management Application

## Overview

A comprehensive application designed to manage personal lending operations, enabling users to track loans, calculate interest, generate reports, and manage repayment schedules across desktop and mobile platforms.

## Functional Specifications

### 1. Borrower Management

- Create and maintain borrower profiles
- Store personal information and contact details
- Update borrower information as needed

### 2. Lending Operations

- Record lending amounts and terms
- Track interest rates and repayment schedules
- Maintain comprehensive lending records

### 3. Interest Calculation

- Automated interest computation based on principal, rate, and schedule
- Support for various interest calculation methods

### 4. Reporting & Analytics

- Generate lending history reports
- View outstanding balances and payment schedules
- Export reports in CSV and PDF formats

### 5. Notifications & Reminders

- Automated reminders for upcoming repayments
- Event notifications (due dates, profile updates)
- Multi-channel notification delivery

### 6. Data Management

- Import borrower and lending data from CSV files
- Export data in common formats (CSV, PDF)
- Automated backup mechanisms
- Data recovery capabilities

### 7. Dashboard

- Overview of total lending activities
- Display outstanding amounts and upcoming repayments
- Summary of key metrics
